Ruru Systems has employees with over 25 years experience in the security and network industry in NZ, South America, USA and Europe.

Ruru (Maori name for "Morepork" - NZ's native owl) are passionate about 3 things - Security/Availability&/Complexity. Ruru understand the interactions of complecity on the fulcrum between security and availability. Ruru believe like the ruru if an enterprise is to succeed you need to be agile, but above all SEE what is happening so that you can react to your environment. This means that security is not about building more fences/barriers but more about knowing(visibility) what is happening. Ruru believe that in the current world environment businesses have consumed vendors security FUD messaging and have lost sight of basic business planing such as matching Business Requirements with Business Risk Analysis. i.e Security is the new "fad" and Business ROI is not being applied because of marketplace "propoganda".

Ruru believe that there is a pendulum between availability and security. Ruru believe that the pendulum has swung to far to the security side where businesses are now bogged down (unavailable) in state/procedures where they have lost any agility chasing a security nirvana that they cant measure whether they have reached

They find themselves now uncompetitive, ponderous behomeths where any IT project runs in over budget, over time and never satisfies even the most basic of requirements.
