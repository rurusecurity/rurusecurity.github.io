Ruru (Maori name for "Morepork" - NZ's native owl) are passionate about 3 things - Security, Availability, & Complexity. Ruru understand the interactions of complexity and its causes as well as the fulcrum between security and availability.

Ruru believe like the Morepork if an enterprise is to succeed it needs to be agile to remain competitive, but above all the enterprise has to SEE what is happening so that it can react to the ever changing business environment.

This means that business security is not about building more fences/barriers but more about knowing (visibility) what is happening so that the business can react (agile anyone?).

A fence is worthless if you dont have visibility of people jumping the fence...

Ruru believe that in the current world environment, businesses have consumed vendors security FUD messaging and have lost sight of basic business planing such as matching Business Requirements with Business Risk Analysis. i.e Security is the new "fad" and Business ROI is not being applied because of marketplace "propoganda".

Ruru believe that there is a pendulum between availability and security. Ruru believe that the pendulum has swung to far to the security side where businesses are now bogged down (unavailable) in state/procedures where they have lost any agility by chasing a security nirvana. The cruel twist of fate is that this security nirvana has left them with an unavailable, useless network and they wouldnt have had the ability to measure if they had reached the nirvana ignoring the possibility that it is unattainable..

They find themselves now uncompetitive, ponderous behomeths where any IT project runs in over budget, over time and never satisfies even the most basic of requirements.

Sound familar?

If so Ruru have abilities to remove your business from the "Security Mire" and make you competitve again.
